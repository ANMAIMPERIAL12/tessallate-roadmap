Tessellate Concept

The Privacy Paradigm Shift

Traditional Collaborative Sharing

  • Centralized Database Clean Rooms: Requires moving all organizations' raw customer data to a single cloud provider.
  • Trust Contracts: Privacy is contractual, not mathematical. If the intermediary is hacked, all raw datasets are compromised.
  • Compliance Liability: Exposing records directly violates HIPAA and GDPR cross-border sharing regulations.

Tessellate Cryptographic Guarantees

  • Decentralized Local Storage: Every node connects locally to its own private database. Raw data never leaves local boundaries.
  • Privacy by Mathematics: Data is encrypted before leaving the node. Multi-party computation handles addition/subtraction on ciphertexts.
  • Strict Audit Logging: Every request is signed and verified across the network, automatically matching local privacy budgets.
